
-----------API Endpoints--------------

1- Donations
GET/api/ - List of all Donations (1)

2- Donors
GET/api/donor/ - List all donors id with their details (2)

3- Campaigns
GET/api/campaign/ - List all campaigns id with their details (3)

GET/api/campaigns/1/ - List Donation amount per currency (4)

GET/api/campaigns/complete/ - List Completed donation per campaign (5)
